We often assume that our cleaning products are keeping our homes safe. However, local and commercially available cleaners often contain hazardous chemicals such as:


Common cleaners contain harmful chemicals like ammonia and sulfur that can cause severe health problems.

  • Ammonia:

     Found in glass cleaners, ammonia exposure can lead to skin irritation and respiratory issues.

  • Glycol ethers:

     Common in multi-surface cleaners, these are known to cause reproductive and developmental issues.

  • VOC Emissions:

     Volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from these products linger in your home, contributing to indoor air pollution.

  • Sulfur and Sulfates:

     These ingredients, while effective in cleaning, often disrupt skin barriers, leading to irritation or allergic reactions.

Prolonged exposure to chemical-laden cleaners can lead to 'Sick Home Syndrome' – where indoor pollutants harm our health.
