Hidden Dangers in Common Cleaners

We often assume our cleaning products are keeping us safe. However, many commercial cleaners contain hazardous chemicals, including:

  • Ammonia: Found in glass cleaners, it can cause skin irritation and respiratory problems.
  • Glycol ethers: Found in multi-surface cleaners, these chemicals are linked to reproductive and developmental issues.
  • VOC Emissions: VOCs from cleaning products linger in the air, contributing to indoor pollution.
  • Sulfur and Sulfates: These can cause skin irritation and allergic reactions.

Prolonged exposure to chemical-laden cleaners can lead to 'Sick Home Syndrome,' where indoor pollutants harm your health.
